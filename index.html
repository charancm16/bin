About AP Police Headquarters

The Andhra Pradesh Police Headquarters is the central command for law enforcement across the state, dedicated to maintaining peace and order.

Our Mission

To protect and serve the citizens of Andhra Pradesh with integrity, professionalism, and respect for human rights.

Our Vision

A safe and secure Andhra Pradesh where citizens can live without fear of crime or violence.

Our Values

Integrity, Accountability, Transparency, Compassion, and Community Partnership guide all our actions.
